Q1 What is GroupFun?

GroupFun is a B to C site which provides a platform for students to join together to get discounted price exclusively for group tickets.

Q2 I like today's deal - how do I get it?

Just click "Buy Now" before the offer ends at midnight.We will charge your card and send you a link to print your coupon.

Q3 Do I need to make use of my deal the same day I buy it?

Quite the contrary, though most coupons have an expiration date.

Q4 I bought a deal - how do I use it?

Once you're charged, you'll receive an e-mail with a link to sign in and print your voucher. It has redemption instructions and a map right on it! You can also print your coupon through the My Orders tab, accessible by clicking My Account in the top right corner once you are logged in.

Q5 I can't login into my account!

Please note that you can subscribe to our newsletters without creating a GroupFun account. Therefore, the fact that you receive our emails does not mean you have registered. If your email address is not recognized, you will have to register to purchase a deal. Click onto “Register” in the top right corner of our website to register as new user.

Q6 I can't remember my password...

Click on the forgotten password link at the login page, and we will send an email to your account-holding email address with a link to reset your password, but there can be a short time-lag before you receive this email.
